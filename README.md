NOIZE / RUNNER

A neon frequency platformer where movement creates music.

NOIZE / RUNNER combines high-speed parkour platforming with generative audio, reactive environments, and a living synthwave universe. Every platform contributes notes, every landing creates rhythm, and every run builds a unique audiovisual experience.

Features

* Fast-paced side-view platforming
* Fluid jump, double-jump, float, and drop mechanics
* Procedurally generated platform layouts
* Reactive musical platform notes
* Frequency modes that alter gameplay physics
* Dynamic synthwave soundtrack generation
* Infinite cyber-space environment
* Neon particle systems and waveform trails
* Mobile-first design optimized for iPhone

Controls

* Tap = Jump
* Air Tap = Double Jump
* Hold = Float
* Swipe Down = Fast Drop
* Tap After Crash = Restart

Frequency Modes

SINE

Long airtime and smooth movement.

SQUARE

Faster sprint speed and heavier gravity.

TRIANGLE

Balanced platforming and rhythm.

NOIZE

High speed, aggressive boosts, and risky jumps.
